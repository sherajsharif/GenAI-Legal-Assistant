Name: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)
Purpose: Affordable housing for urban and rural poor.
Eligibility: Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Low Income Groups (LIG), and Middle-Income Groups (MIG).
Link: https://pmaymis.gov.in/

---

Name: Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)
Purpose: Financial inclusion by opening zero-balance bank accounts.
Eligibility: All citizens aged 10 and above.
Link: https://pmjdy.gov.in/

---

Name: Startup India Scheme
Purpose: To support innovation and startups through funding, tax benefits, and regulatory support.
Eligibility: Startups recognized by DPIIT, established after 2016.
Link: https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

---

Name: Stand-Up India Scheme
Purpose: Promote entrepreneurship among women and SC/ST.
Eligibility: SC/ST or women entrepreneurs aged 18+, seeking loan between ₹10–100 lakhs.
Link: https://www.standupmitra.in/

---

Name: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana
Purpose: Provide income support to farmers.
Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land.
Link: https://pmkisan.gov.in/
